Gulab Jamun

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Gulab Jamun Powder
  • Oil

Recipe

  1. firstly, in a large bowl take ¾ cup milk powder, ½ cup maida and ½ tsp baking powder.
  2. mix well, homemade gulab jamun mix is ready
  3. meanwhile, prepare the sugar syrup by taking 2 cup sugar, 2 cup water, 2 cardamom and ¼ tsp saffron.
  4. make sure there are no cracks in the jamun. if there are cracks then there are high chances for jamuns to break while frying.

Malai Barfi

Ingredients

  • Sugar
  • Milk and Cream
  • Dry Fruits

Recipe

  1. now add 1 cup condensed milk and mix well. do not add any extra sugar as condensed milk contains sweetness.
  2. crumble the paneer prepared. you can also grate store brought paneer
  3. mset well forming a block. top it with chopped cashew nuts, almonds and press gently.
  4. finally, serve malai burfi or store in an airtight container for a week in the refrigerator.
